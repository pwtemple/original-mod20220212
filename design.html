Design & Planning

Design Service

It is very rare to undertake any building project without changes being required during works.

Be it a client simply wishing to change something as works progress or a structural reason which may warrant a change being made.

As such items arise during works we have developed an ability to deal with them as efficiently as possible and from this our design service has evolved.

Computer Drawing Systems

Working mostly in old buildings we had for many years been simply making things work.

With our design service progressing well we invested in a computer drawing system and training, arriving at where we are today offering a totally bespoke design service.

 

Survey - Design - Planning

An average project will include a full site survey and drawings of the building as existing.

We then discuss the requirements and see what we can make work based around our clientsâ€™ wishes and the technical wishes and the technical requirements of building control.

When a proposal is agreed upon we  then discuss it with the planning department and we submit a planning application.  At this stage we can then work on detailed drawings and produce a quotation for the works for our client.

If clients has their own preferred building contractor which they wish to use we are happy to carry out the design only or alternatively design and also planning application.
