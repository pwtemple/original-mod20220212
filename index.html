P.W. Temple - Specialists in

Restoration & Renovation

Situated on the northern edge of the

Lake District national park, PW Temple Ltd has been renovating and restoring property in the surrounding area for over thirty five years.

Specialising in the renovation of existing buildings, PW Temple Ltd has become an

expert in restoring property, attuning

 it to its surroundings and making the most of existing features.

Quality craftsmanship, design and service are core values that have driven the

company to achieve its excellent reputation, along with a number of local and national awards.

AWARDS

Master Builder of the Year

Prof. Bob Fowler and his wife Penny entered their renovated studio into the Federation of Master Builders, Master Builder of the year competition.

The entry went on to win the national award for best renovation. The project also won the Building Control award for Built in Quality.

            Peter Temple's special quality is a rare ability to take the problems of the client and provide creative, workable solutions and to execute them within the cost and time agreed. He is a Master Builder who inspires confidence in the client and in his team and is the model of a professional.

Prof. Bob Fowler
