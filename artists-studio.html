Artists Studio

Penny Fowler read an article in the local paper, The Keswick Reminder, about ourselves.  We had won a regional award for a renovation project in the Federation of Master Builders ‘Master Builder of the Year’ competition.

Penny had planning consent to fully convert the former cattle housing into an artist’s studio and contacted us.  We were pleased to be asked to look at a project which we knew we could do well.

On completion it took the national award in the same category, also winning the Building Control award for ‘Built

in Quality’.

                Since its completion the Gutherscale Studio on Catbells has become the envy of visiting artists and the thoughtful planning and provision of facilities - kitchen shower/bathroom- in addition to the fabulous painting and hanging space - have heightened and enriched the experience for visitors and guests.

The Studio has happily ‘come of age’ and throughout it's first decade has had the benefit of regular visits from Peter Temple’s always reliable team, there in rain or shine whenever needed. Master builders indeed!

Prof. Bob Fowler
